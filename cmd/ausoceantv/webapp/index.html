What is AusOceanTV?

AusOceanTV is a live streaming platform that showcases the beauty of Australia's oceans. Our underwater cameras are deployed in various locations around Australia, capturing the marine life in real time.

Underwater Cameras

AusOceanTV utilises custom underwater cameras which are designed, tested, and built by the AusOcean team. Our cameras are engineered specifically for continuous underwater streaming, bringing the highest quality footage live to AusOceanTV.
